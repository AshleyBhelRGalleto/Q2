Reasons for Inequality in Education

  • Poverty and limited resources
  • Geographical disparities
  • Social and gender biases
  • Lack of infrastructure and access to schools

Effects of Poor Education

  • Limited job opportunities and lower income
  • Increased inequality and social unrest
  • Impaired personal and community development
